Purchase Loans

Your path to homeownership starts here

Whether you're a first-time buyer or looking to move up, we have the perfect loan program to help you purchase your dream home with competitive rates and flexible terms.

Available Programs:

Conventional Loans

  • Down payment as low as 3%
  • Fixed and adjustable rates
  • Loan amounts up to $766,550

FHA Loans

  • Down payment as low as 3.5%
  • Flexible credit requirements

VA Loans

  • No down payment required
  • No private mortgage insurance

USDA Loans

  • No down payment in eligible areas
  • Low mortgage insurance

  • Income restrictions apply

Jumbo Loans

Financing for luxury homes and high-value properties

For properties that exceed conventional loan limits, our jumbo loan programs provide competitive financing for luxury homes and high-value properties with flexible terms and attractive rates.

Jumbo Loan Features:

High Loan Amounts

Loans from $766,551 up to $3 million or more for qualified borrowers

Competitive Rates

Attractive interest rates that often compete with conventional loans

Flexible Terms

15, 20, or 30-year terms with fixed and adjustable rate options

Portfolio Options

Special programs for investment properties and portfolio lending

Qualification Requirements:

Credit Score

Typically 700+ for best rates

Down Payment

Usually 10-20% minimum

Debt-to-Income

Generally 43% or lower

Reserves

2-6 months of payments
