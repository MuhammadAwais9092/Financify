Common Questions

Quick answers to frequently asked questions

How long does the mortgage process take?

Typically 30-45 days from application to closing, but we can often close in as little as 15 days for qualified borrowers.

What credit score do I need?

Credit requirements vary by loan type. We work with borrowers with credit scores as low as 580 for FHA loans.

How much do I need for a down payment?

Down payments can be as low as 0% for VA loans, 3.5% for FHA loans, and 3% for conventional loans.

Can I get pre-approved online?

Yes! Our digital application process allows you to get pre-approved quickly and securely online.
