Calculator Tips & Information

Understanding your mortgage calculations for better financial decisions

Down Payment

A larger down payment reduces your loan amount and can help you avoid PMI. Conventional loans typically require 3-20% down, while FHA loans may allow as little as 3.5%.

Interest Rates

Your interest rate depends on your credit score, down payment, loan term, and current market conditions. Even a small rate difference can significantly impact your monthly payment.

PMI Insurance

Private Mortgage Insurance is typically required when your down payment is less than 20%. PMI can usually be removed once you reach 20% equity in your home.

Debt-to-Income Ratio

Lenders typically prefer a debt-to-income ratio of 43% or less. This includes your new mortgage payment plus all other monthly debt obligations.
